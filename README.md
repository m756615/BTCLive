# BTCLive Pro v1.5.0

**Real-time Bitcoin Price Tracker for your Android Status Bar.**

BTCLive Pro is a professional-grade Android utility that puts the live price of Bitcoin directly next to your system clock. It's designed for power users who need second-by-second updates without constantly switching apps.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Status Bar Overlay**: See the BTC price at the very top of your screen, next to the time.
- **5-Second Updates**: Ultra-fast market data refreshes every 5 seconds.
- **Price Alerts**: Set target prices and receive high-priority system-wide popups.
- **52 Sorted Colors**: Customizing the price text with pastels, ultra-brights, and grayscale.
- **Dynamic Contrast**: Automatically switches color for best readability on any wallpaper.
- **Cloud Backup**: Export/Import your settings and alert history via JSON.
- **Lock Screen Support**: Persistent notification ensures you see the price even when locked.
- **Start-on-Boot**: Automatically resumes tracking after a phone restart.

## 🛠 Setup Instructions
1. **Grant Permissions**: The app requires "Display over other apps" and "Notification" access.
2. **Battery Optimization**: Set the app to "Unrestricted" in Android Settings > Apps > BTCLive > Battery to ensure the 5-second updates run perfectly in the background.
3. **Price Alerts**: Add your targets in the Alerts menu to get instant popups.
